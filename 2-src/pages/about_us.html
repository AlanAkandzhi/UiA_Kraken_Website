About Us

We, UIA Kraken, are based at the University of Agder, Grimstad Campus in Southern Norway. Kraken was founded through the Academic E-sports program (link here).

The bachelor’s programme introduces the world of academic esports and develops skills for a much broader field. This means that in addition to the activity of exercise, you learn about training, recovery, and performance.

You will also learn about the role of esports in society, technology related to esports, and how to use esports in education and in professional life.

The sixth semester is dedicated to writing a bachelor thesis. The students will then focus on one of the following four specialisations in the field:

  • Esports athlete, coach, or managing
  • The industry and ecosystem related to esports
  • Technology and innovation
  • Education and healthcare

This is just the beginning; we are expanding more and more, providing opportunities for aspiring esports enthusiasts!

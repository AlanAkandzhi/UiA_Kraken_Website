Our Teams

At Kraken.gg, we have competitive teams striving to perform at the highest level, as well as casual teams for players who want to have fun with friends. Our A and B teams are coached by experienced professionals and compete in major tournaments, while our C and D teams are all about having a good time and building friendships.

Currently, we have teams for popular games like League of Legends, Valorant, Rocket League, and many more. We’re also looking to expand into new games and bring even more players under the Kraken banner!

Kraken A Team

The Kraken A Team competes in top-tier leagues and tournaments across different games, showcasing our talent and dedication to esports. With a rigorous training schedule and top-notch coaching, these players are constantly pushing the limits.

Kraken B Team

The Kraken B Team serves as the backbone for our competitive roster. These players are constantly developing their skills, participating in competitive games, and aiming to break into the A Team. They bring determination and drive to everything they do.

Kraken C & D Teams

Our C and D teams are open to players who love gaming and want to socialize. These teams are perfect for players looking to join a community, make new friends, and play without the pressure of high-stakes competition.
